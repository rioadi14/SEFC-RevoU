About Us

ZAIA Enterprise Japan alternatively referred to as ZAIA Japan or simply ZAIA, was a subsidiary of ZAIA Enterprise based in Japan. In addition to marketing products for civilians, they were also a weapons developer.

ZAIA Japan was involved with the production of the prototype Humagears. We also supply A.I.M.S. with their equipment and Progrisekeys.
